LunaLog Privacy Policy
Last Updated: November 18, 2025

Introduction
LunaLog ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the LunaLog iOS application.

Information We Collect
Information You Provide
Journal Entries:

Titles and content of your journal entries
Dates and times you create or modify entries
This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to us
Authentication Information:

When you sign in with Apple, we receive:
A unique user identifier
Your name (if you choose to share it)
Your email address (or Apple's private relay email if you choose to hide your email)
This information is used solely for authentication purposes
Automatically Collected Information
Lunar Phase Data:

The app fetches current moon phase information from MoonAPI (https://rapidapi.com/MoonAPIcom/api/moon-phase/)
This request includes:
Current date
No personal information is sent to MoonAPI
Data is cached locally for 6 hours to minimize API calls
Device Information:

The app may collect basic device information for crash reporting and analytics (if implemented in future versions)
Currently, no analytics or tracking is implemented
How We Use Your Information
Journal Data:

Your journal entries are stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account
We have zero access to your journal content
Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud infrastructure
Authentication:

Sign in with Apple credentials are used only to:
Authenticate your identity
Associate your journal data with your iCloud account
Display your name in the app settings
Lunar Data:

Moon phase information is used to:
Display current lunar context in the app
Associate journal entries with lunar phases
Enhance your journaling experience
Data Storage and Security
iCloud Storage:

All journal entries are stored in your personal iCloud account
Data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit
Storage is subject to Apple's iCloud Terms of Service
We do not have access to your iCloud data
Local Storage:

The app caches lunar phase data locally for performance
Authentication tokens are stored securely in iOS Keychain
No sensitive data is stored unencrypted
Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

Apple Services:

iCloud storage and sync (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
Sign in with Apple authentication (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
MoonAPI:

Current date is sent to fetch lunar data
No personal or identifying information is shared
Subject to MoonAPI's terms and privacy policy
Your Rights
Access and Control:

You control all your journal data through your iCloud account
You can export, delete, or modify entries at any time within the app
You can sign out of the app at any time from Settings
Data Deletion:

Deleting entries in the app removes them from your iCloud storage
Deleting the app does not automatically delete your iCloud data
To permanently delete all data:
Delete all entries within the app, or
Manage iCloud data through iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage
iCloud Management:

You can disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings
When disabled, data remains local to your device only
Children's Privacy
LunaLog is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Services

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

Updating the "Last Updated" date
Posting the new policy in the app (if applicable)
Sending notification through TestFlight (for beta testers)
Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention
Journal entries: Retained indefinitely in your iCloud until you delete them
Authentication data: Retained until you sign out or delete the app
Cached lunar data: Automatically expires after 6 hours
International Users
LunaLog uses iCloud, which stores data in data centers around the world. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations, subject to Apple's data protection standards.
